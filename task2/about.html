About Al Baik Restaurant

Al Baik is a fast-food restaurant chain founded in the mid-1970s in Jeddah. Saudi Arabia, by Shukor Abu Ghazaleh. It is famous for its chicken and seafood meals with potatoes , known as broasted chicken.. The Al Baik meal became very popular and well-received due to its distinctive taste resulting from the mixture of spices and seasonings used.
